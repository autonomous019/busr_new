Busr: Smart Way Finding for Transit

Commuting Problems

80% of the population of New York Metro area commutes regularly each day using public transit. Causing bottlenecks, delays and confusion in commuting. With very little intelligent tools to address issues of making commuting easier.

Analytics

Traffic Flow analysis provides intelligent way finding in the white noise of transportation big data. Through using tools such as SUMO and Open Trip Planner and other methods smart decisions about way finding can be made.

Solutions

Busr App allows to integrate real-time data from multiple sources to update transportation information on the fly. Available on Web, iOS and Android.
