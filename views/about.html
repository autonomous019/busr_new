Busr: Smart Way Finding

Busr is a start-up project of Enneagineering that has a front end consumer facing transit information service with transit tools to ease public transit and general commuting problems. Behind the scenes it provides a means for profiling traffic and urban environments to find efficient pathways for way finding that has larger commercial applications. This app will be on the web using Node.js/HTML5/Redis; on iOS as an app for iPhone/Pod/Pad; and on Android. It incorporates programming in Javascript, Ruby, Python, Java, Objective-C and C++ languages. For more info contact: Mikey McCarron 415-323-3648
